Schneider Electric

One of the world’s largest electrical manufacturers needed to replace their outdated estimating software.

I was appointed as the lead UX/UI designer and product manager for this challenging project, overseeing a team of four developers.

Project goal

The goal of this project was to replace an outdated lotus notes enterprise estimating software, used by over 200 field service engineers with an Ipad and web app version. It needed to help the department increase upsell, improve accuracy and traceability. Most importantly it needed to as frictionless as possible as engineers had in the past chosen not to bother quoting as the old system was slow, hard to use and often wrong.

Research methods

Before software development started I met with stakeholders to understand business logic but more importantly I met with the engineers and admin staff that actually use the software to find out how the current system made them feel applying the following research methods:

  • Detailed interviews
  • User testing
  • Personas
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Information architecture
  • Market research analysis
